  • General supervision and daily monitoring of residents' abilities and needs. Includes preparedness to intervene in crisis and assist with resident self-administration of medication when required.

Meet Raluca

Primary Caregiver

After working as a Care Provider in her family's business, Raluca Covaciu discovered her passion for working with elderly people and creating the most loving and caring environment possible for them. Raluca went on to establish Heritage Assisted Living at Northerra as a way of giving back and showing appreciation for the lives of her patients. Raluca and her staff work hard to ensure that the family members have little to worry about, leaving more time to focus on enjoying their loved ones. To Raluca, once a patient moves in, they instantly become a member of the family.
